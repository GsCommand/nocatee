Pool deck care

Wet areas require deliberate preparation and finish choices.

Screen enclosures, shaded cage lines, deck drains, and frequent splash-out create conditions that differ from a driveway. We inspect organic growth, weak joints, existing coatings, and water movement before recommending restoration.

The deck is cleaned with attention to coping and adjacent surfaces, then allowed to dry before sanding and sealing. The goal is a consistent, refreshed pool area without masking drainage problems or over-applying product.

  1. Inspect: review coping, drains, cage edges, stains, and prior sealer.
  2. Clean: treat buildup and prepare the paver surface.
  3. Restore: replace depleted joint material where the deck needs it.
  4. Finish: seal under suitable surface and weather conditions.
