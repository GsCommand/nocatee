Process

No shortcut sealing.

Bad paver sealing usually comes from rushing: sealing wet pavers, skipping sand issues, ignoring stains, or applying the wrong finish for Florida conditions. This site should be positioned around process, not cheap pressure washing.

  1. Inspect: check sand loss, stains, moisture, drainage, and surface condition.
  2. Clean: wash the surface and treat organic buildup where needed.
  3. Sand: refill joints for a tighter, cleaner, more stable surface.
  4. Seal: apply sealer when weather and moisture conditions are right.
