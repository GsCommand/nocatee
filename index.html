Questions

Nocatee paver sealing questions

How much does paver sealing cost in Nocatee, FL?

Concrete and brick pavers start at $1.50 per square foot; travertine and natural stone are $1.60. Stripping a failed sealer adds $1.50 per square foot, efflorescence treatment adds $0.05, and releveling is $8 per paver. A typical 600 to 1,000 square foot driveway runs $900 to $1,500; a 400 to 800 square foot pool deck runs $600 to $1,300.

How often should pavers be sealed here?

Every three to five years for most Nocatee surfaces. Full-sun driveways and pool decks with constant water exposure sit at the short end; covered lanais last longer. Builder-applied sealer is usually one thin coat and typically starts failing in year three.

Why is my driveway turning orange?

Iron from reclaimed irrigation water. JEA supplies reclaimed water throughout Nocatee and it carries more dissolved mineral than potable water. Sprinkler overspray deposits iron on the pavers, where it oxidizes into rust arcs. Pressure washing will not remove it and sealing over it makes it permanent — it needs a dedicated rust treatment first. See the driveway sealing page.

Do you seal travertine and pool decks too?

Yes. Travertine sealing uses stone-safe chemistry and a breathable penetrating sealer — never acid and never a film. Pool deck sealing is traction-first and planned around screen enclosures and salt systems. We also seal patios, walkways, retaining walls, columns and fire pits.

Will sealing make my pavers slippery?

Not when applied correctly. Slick surfaces come from over-application, the wrong product for the material, or a high-gloss finish chosen for looks. We apply controlled even coats and prioritize footing on anything walked on barefoot.

How long before I can park on the driveway?

Dry to the touch in one to two hours, light foot traffic in two to four. We ask for 24 hours before regular foot traffic and 48 to 72 hours before vehicles. Humidity, shade and overcast weather extend both.

Do you use polymeric sand?

Generally no. In Florida heat and humidity polymeric sand can trap moisture and fail early. We use Trident ASTM C144 kiln-dried joint sand compacted to full depth with a joint-stabilizing sealer over the top.

Can you fix sunken or rocking pavers?

Yes, at $8 per paver, and we do it before sealing rather than sealing over an unstable surface. Sealing is a surface system — it does not correct base settlement or drainage problems, and we will tell you if that is what you are actually looking at.

Do you pressure wash the house and roof as well?

Yes. Soft wash house washing covers stucco, siding, trim, soffits, fascia and exterior gutter faces. Roof washing uses soft-wash chemistry for algae and black streaking, never high pressure. Most homeowners book exterior washing in the same visit as paver sealing.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes — licensed, insured, and a Trident Master Certified paver sealing applicator. Every paver sealing project carries a written 2-year warranty.
