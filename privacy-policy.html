Privacy Policy

This policy explains how HydroSeal and the Nocatee Paver Sealing website collect, use, retain and share information when you request an estimate, send project photos, call, text, email or use embedded website tools.

Information you provide

You may provide your name, telephone number, email address, service address, neighborhood, project measurements, preferred contact method, scheduling details, messages and photos or videos of the property. Do not send sensitive financial, medical or identity information through a quote form or chatbot.

Information collected automatically

Hosting, security, analytics and embedded-service providers may receive basic device, browser, IP address, page-view, referral and interaction information. Cookies or similar technologies may be used to operate forms, remember settings, measure performance, prevent abuse and understand how visitors use the site.

How information is used

  • Review and price requested work.
  • Contact you about estimates, scheduling, service and follow-up.
  • Maintain project, warranty, accounting and customer-service records.
  • Operate, secure and improve the website.
  • Comply with legal, insurance, tax and safety requirements.

Text messages and calls

When you provide a phone number or initiate a call or text, HydroSeal may respond about your inquiry or project. Message and data rates may apply. You can ask not to receive nonessential follow-up messages, but transactional communication may still be needed for active estimates or scheduled work.

Project photographs

Photos you submit are used to evaluate the project. HydroSeal will not intentionally publish customer-submitted photos that clearly identify a person, address, vehicle plate or private information without permission. Completed-project images may be used for records, warranty review or marketing when permitted.

Third-party services

The site may use hosting, quote-form, analytics, review, gallery, chatbot, communications or map providers. Information processed through those tools may be governed by the provider's terms and privacy practices. HydroSeal does not control every third-party service or website linked from this site.

Sharing and sale of information

HydroSeal does not sell personal information. Information may be shared with service providers that support website hosting, communications, scheduling, payments, accounting, insurance or legal compliance, and when reasonably necessary to protect customers, the business or others.

Retention

Quote and project information may be retained for customer service, warranty, tax, insurance, dispute and recordkeeping purposes. Information that is no longer reasonably needed may be deleted or de-identified, subject to backup and legal-retention limitations.

Security

Reasonable administrative and technical safeguards are used, but no website, email, text message or online storage system can guarantee absolute security. Use ordinary caution when sending property details or images.

Your choices

You may request access, correction or deletion of information HydroSeal controls. Some records may be retained when required for contracts, warranty administration, accounting, insurance, safety or legal obligations.

Contact

Questions or requests may be sent to info@hydrosealpavers.com or 904-537-5000.
