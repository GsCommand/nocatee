Driveway renewal

A durable finish starts with the joints and the drainage.

Nocatee driveways collect tire residue, irrigation deposits, and windblown sand along the curb. Before sealing, we assess worn joints, settled areas, stains, and how water leaves the surface so preparation can match the driveway’s actual condition.

Cleaning and fresh joint sand create the foundation for an even sealer application. Minor repair needs are identified before the finish is applied, and drying conditions are checked rather than relying on a fixed timeline.

  1. Evaluate: document stains, loose pavers, joint loss, and drainage.
  2. Prepare: clean the field, borders, and street-facing edge.
  3. Restore: complete agreed repairs and replenish appropriate joints.
  4. Seal: apply the selected finish when the prepared surface is ready.
