Questions

Nocatee roof washing questions

Do you pressure wash roof shingles?

No. HydroSeal uses a soft-wash roof cleaning process designed to treat organic staining without relying on aggressive pressure. The roof material and condition are inspected before service.

What causes black streaks on a roof?

Black roof streaks are commonly associated with organic growth that thrives in Florida's humid conditions. A project inspection helps distinguish treatable buildup from wear, damage, or discoloration that cleaning cannot correct.

How do you protect landscaping during roof washing?

HydroSeal plans the application and runoff around plants and exterior surfaces, uses reasonable protective measures, and monitors the work area. The exact process depends on roof access, drainage, weather, and the property layout.

How long does a roof stay clean after soft washing?

Results vary with shade, tree cover, humidity, roof material, drainage, and surrounding conditions. Roof washing removes current organic buildup but cannot prevent all future growth.
